VISION

The Tech National High School envisions
to produce graduates who are prepared for
higher education, morally and spiritually
upright, and productive citizens who will
contribute in the development of the
community where they live.

MISSION

Towards the realization of her vision,
the Mankayan National
High School is commited to:

    1. Instruction necessary for the
    intellectual, moral, social, physical and mental
    development to prepare
    them for higher education;

    2. Promote high dedication and
    competence of service among school
    personnel;

    3. Provide adequate and updated facilities
    and equipments; and

    4. Creare a warm, caring, safe and positive
    educational environment.

The first secondary school in Baguio was the normal school in 1916 later called Baguio Tech National High School. Classes were held where Home Sweet Home now stands as this area and the present University of the Philippines Baguio location was included in the reserved land for the Bureau of Education by Forbes. In 1919 it became Mountain Province High School.
